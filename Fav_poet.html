NASIR KAZMI

1925 - 1972

One of the founders of modern ghazal. Born at Ambala in India, he migrated to Pakistan and wrote extensively on the pain and sufferings of partition.

Niyat e shauk bhar na jae kahi
tu bhi dil se utar na jae kahi

aaj dekhā hai tujh ko der ke ba.ad
aaj kā din guzar na jaa.e kahīñ

aarzū hai ki tū yahāñ aa.e
aur phir umr bhar na jaa.e kahīñ
